Live The Adventure!

Fantasy Quest is a unique "living theater" game that has been entertaining audiences for over 30 years. Established in 1993 by a team of passionate medieval fantasy enthusiasts with extensive experience in theater and role-playing games, our goal is to provide an unparalleled live-action role-playing (LARP) experience. At Fantasy Quest, we prioritize friendship, fun, and adventure, creating a one-of-a-kind form of dramatic entertainment in the classic medieval fantasy style. We focus on immersive role-playing, valuing character development and storytelling over hack-and-slash combat. In our world, magic is commonplace, and mythical creatures come to life. We are dedicated to creating a fair and safe environment that challenges creativity and enhances the imagination. Our events are open to individuals ages 16 and up, with ages 16 and 17 requiring written permission from a parent and proof of age. Our events are held at Camp Asto Wamah in Columbia, CT, offering a beautiful setting for a weekend of adventure. Come join us in the Burgh of New Stonewatch for a unique and unforgettable experience. We can't wait to see you there!
